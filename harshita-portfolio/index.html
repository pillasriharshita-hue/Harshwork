Hey, this is Harshita.

This is Harshita. She's a Product Designer who approaches digital products as living ecosystems, shaping end-to-end experiences from the ground up. With a Master's in Human-Computer Interaction and a background in architecture, she bridges the gap between high-level spatial strategy and the granular details of a seamless interface. To her, product design is about the whole journey, ensuring every interaction feels as natural and intentional as a well-planned building.

She also has a soft spot for whimsical things 🧚, greens 🌱, and sunsets 🌇.
