The Origin Story

Sri Harshita Pilla

She's a Product Designer who approaches digital products as living ecosystems, shaping end-to-end user experiences from the ground up. With a Master's in Human-Computer Interaction from DePaul University and a background in architecture, she bridges the gap between high-level spatial strategy and the granular details of a seamless interface.

To her, product design is about the whole journey — ensuring every interaction feels as natural and intentional as a well-planned building.

She also has a soft spot for whimsical things 🧚, greens 🌱, and sunsets 🌇.

03 Design Philosophy
🔬

Research First

Every design decision starts with understanding the human behind the screen. Assumptions are hypotheses, not answers.

✨

Craft Matters

The details aren't just details — they are the design. Pixel-perfect execution and thoughtful micro-interactions make the difference.

🧬

Design for Behavior

Great products don't just look good — they guide behavior. I design with cognitive biases, mental models, and real-world context in mind.
