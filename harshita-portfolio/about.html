About Me

I'm a product designer passionate about research-driven design and behavioral change. I combine deep user understanding with strategic thinking to craft experiences that feel natural, intuitive, and delightful.

Who I am

My Story

I'm Sri Harshita Pilla, a product designer with 3+ years of experience designing end-to-end user experiences from research to launch. Based in the United States, I specialize in turning complex problems into intuitive, human-centered solutions.

My approach is research-first. Before designing, I invest time understanding user needs, motivations, and behaviors through interviews, testing, and data. This deep empathy informs every design decision—from interaction patterns to visual hierarchy.

I thrive at the intersection of design and behavioral change. I'm fascinated by how subtle design nudges can encourage better habits, how information architecture shapes understanding, and how craft elevates an experience. I believe design should be both beautiful and purposeful.
